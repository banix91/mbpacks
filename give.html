Donations for Mercy Backpack

Donations are welcomed. The men and women served by the University of Maryland Violence Prevention Program are often in need of basic toiletries upon thier release from the hospital. Please know there is an ongoing need for all the items listed below. Consider joining us as we strive to provide pratical assistance to Victims of gun violence in the Baltimore area.

Items Needed for Mercy Backpacks

  • Bibles(Any translation)
  • Gospel Tracts
  • Backpacks (Good condtion)
  • Soap(Bars)
  • Deodorant
  • Toothbrushes
  • Toothpaste
  • Toliet Paper
  • Skull Caps(Winter)
  • Gloves(Winter)
  • Disposable Razor Blades
  • Shaving Cream
  • Body Lotion
  • Hand Warmers
  • Hand Sanitizer
  • Sweat Socks
  • Disposable Face Mask
